Peach Upside-Down Cake

YIELDS

8 servings

PREP TIME

0 hrs 8 mins

TOTAL TIME

0 hrs 50 mins

INGREDIENTS

  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 3/4 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp bourbon
  • 3 large peaches, pitted and cut into 1/2' slices
  • 1 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp kosher salt
  • 3/4 cup whole milk
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 large egg
